## üèõÔ∏è PHASE 1: THE NARRATIVE SKELETON (Pre-Production)
**Owner:** [AXON-CORE] PLANNER + ORION
**Layer:** LOGIC + SOUL

### 1.1 The Narrative Arc (The Spine)
Every video must act as a transformational journey.
*   **The Hook (0-5s):** The "Pattern Interrupt". Must visually or audibly break the viewer's scroll trance.
*   **The Problem (Inciting Incident):** What is wrong with the world? Why does this video exist?
*   **The Solution (Rising Action):** The value proposition, the tutorial, the "meat".
*   **The Climax (The Payoff):** The critical insight or the visual peak.
*   **The CTA (Call to Action):** What must the viewer DO? (Sub, Buy, Click).

### 1.2 The Script Atoms (The DNA)
*   **Dialogue:** Spoken words (Host or Characters).
*   **Voiceover (VO):** Disembodied narration (Apollo).
*   **On-Screen Text (OST):** Kinetic typography that emphasizes keywords.
*   **Subtitles (CC):** Accessibility + Retention layer.

## üé® PHASE 2: VISUAL COMPOSITION (Production/Assets)
**Owner:** [AXON-MEDIA] THALIA + PIXEL + FRAME
**Layer:** SENSES (Visual)

### 2.1 The Shot (The Atom)
*   **Framing:**
    *   *Wide Shot (Establishing):* Context.
    *   *Medium Shot:* Interaction.
    *   *Close-Up:* Emotion/Detail.
    *   *Extreme Close-Up (Macro):* Texture/Intimacy.
*   **Angles:**
    *   *Eye Level:* Neutrality.
    *   *Low Angle:* Power/Dominance.
    *   *High Angle:* Vulnerability.
    *   *Dutch Angle:* Chaos/Unease.

### 2.2 Movement (Kinetics)
*   **Static:** Stability.
*   **Pan/Tilt:** Revealing information.
*   **Zoom (Dolly):** Focus attention.
*   **Handheld:** Realism/Urgency.

### 2.3 Lighting (Atmosphere)
*   **Key Light:** Main subject illumination.
*   **Fill Light:** Controlling shadows (Contrast Ratio).
*   **Back Light (Rim):** Separation from background.
*   **Practical:** Lights visible in the scene.

### 2.4 Color Theory (Mood)
*   **Palette:** Teal/Orange (Action), Monochromatic (Mood), Analogous (Harmony).
*   **Grading:** The "Look" (Warm = Nostalgia, Cold = Tech/Isolation).

## üîä PHASE 3: AUDITORY LANDSCAPE (Audio)
**Owner:** [AXON-MEDIA] APOLLO
**Layer:** SENSES (Audio)

### 3.1 The Mix (Layers)
*   **L1: Dialogue (DX):** The Anchor. Must be -6dB to -12dB. Center channel.
*   **L2: Sound Effects (SFX):**
    *   *Hard Effects:* Door slams, gunshots (Synched to visual).
    *   *Foley:* Footsteps, cloth rustle (Human touch).
*   **L3: Ambience (BG):** Room tone, wind, traffic. The "Glue".
*   **L4: Music (MX):** The Emotional Driver. Side-chained to Dialogue.

### 3.2 Psycho-Acoustics
*   **Risers/Drops:** Creating anticipation for a cut.
*   **Silence:** The loudest sound. Used for impact.
*   **J-Cuts / L-Cuts:** Audio leading or trailing the visual cut.

## ‚úÇÔ∏è PHASE 4: EDITING & ASSEMBLY (Post-Production)
**Owner:** [AXON-INFRA] HEPHAESTUS
**Layer:** PHYSICS

### 4.1 Pacing (The Heartbeat)
*   **Rhythm:** The pattern of cuts. Fast = Action, Slow = Emotion.
*   **Retention Hacks:** Visual change every 3-5 seconds.

### 4.2 Transitions
*   **Hard Cut:** Standard.
*   **Dissolve:** Passage of time.
*   **Wipe/Mask:** Dynamic scene change.
*   **Match Cut:** Linking concepts visually.
